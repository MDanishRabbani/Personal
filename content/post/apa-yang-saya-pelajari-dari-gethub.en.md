Gethub was one of the projects that taught me the most about turning an idea into something people can actually use. The project came from the Bangkit Academy Mobile Development Cohort and focused on connecting project owners with freelancers.

<!--more-->

## What I Learned

- A good product is not just a long feature list; the flow has to make sense from start to finish.
- Integrating OCR, sentiment analysis, chat, and escrow payment requires clean data structure and clear status handling.
- When a system serves real users, small details such as identity verification and user trust matter a lot.

## Why It Matters

Gethub showed me that full-stack work is not only about building interfaces and APIs. It is also about combining business needs, user experience, and technical constraints into one coherent flow.
