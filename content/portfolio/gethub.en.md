Gethub is a Bangkit Academy Mobile Development Cohort capstone project led by Google, Tokopedia, Gojek, and Traveloka. The project was designed to connect project owners and freelancers in a more structured way.

<!--more-->

## Main Features

- Freelancer recommendations for project owners
- OCR-based identity verification
- AI-powered sentiment analysis for reviews
- Escrow payment flow
- User-to-user chat
- Digital portfolio
- Business card scanning

## Why It Matters

The project represents a mix of full-stack work, product thinking, and feature integration that can support real operational workflows.
